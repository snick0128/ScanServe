## ‚úÖ THE SOLUTION (Simple & Non-Technical)

ScanServe turns every table into a self-service ordering station. Customers scan a QR code, browse the menu on their phone, and place orders directly. The kitchen sees orders instantly on a digital display, staff track table status in real-time, and owners get complete insights into their business‚Äîall without installing any apps.

**How It Works:**
1. **Customer scans** QR code at their table
2. **Browses menu** with photos, descriptions, and prices
3. **Places order** directly from their phone
4. **Kitchen receives** order on digital display (KDS)
5. **Staff serves** when items are ready
6. **Customer requests** bill and pays
7. **Owner reviews** sales and performance analytics
